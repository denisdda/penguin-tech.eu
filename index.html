Tornador with washing vacuum cleaner adapter

Tornador surpasses all cleaning vacuum cleaners in cleaning efficiency. The quality and ease of cleaning with this device are unrivaled. In this device, the knocking force and the pulling force have been combined by connecting your vacuum cleaner for wet.

Tornador complete with a removable attachment for connecting a washing vacuum cleaner. An excellent option for masters providing dry cleaning services and Detailing centers

To connect and operate, you need a compressor and a washing vacuum cleaner.

The Device can be used both for wet cleaning with a liquid supply and for dry dust removal

The device is connected to an air compressor 0.4-0.9 bar (optimal working pressure 7 bar, it should not exceed 10 bar) and a washing vacuum cleaner. The cleaning liquid is sprayed into the surface under the action of blows of air and the dirt is sucked out by the vacuum cleaner.

The Device can be used both for wet cleaning with a liquid supply and for dry dust removal

Instructions:

1) The device is connected to a compressor with a pressure of 6 to 9 atmospheres. Air consumption 250 liters per minute.
2) A chemical composition for cleaning is poured into the tank of the Tornador.
3) Under the pressure of the compressor, the liquid mixes with the air, forming something like a small tornado.
4) The resulting dirt and liquid gets into the vacuum cleaner, so in a matter of minutes you get a clean surface.
